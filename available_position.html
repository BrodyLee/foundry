Daytime Team Member- Front of House

We are seeking friendly, outgoing and dedicated applicants with a servant's heart to work at Chick-fil-A Seneca in Seneca, SC. Your Chick-fil-A career is a stepping-stone to a successful future in the world of customer service and business.

A Day-time Team Member is responsible in one or multiple areas including:

  • Front Counter &/or Drive-Thru Order Taker
  • Window &/or Front Counter Cashier
  • Front Counter &/or Drive-Thru Expeditor
  • Dining Room Host/Hostess

Shifts available - Morning (7am-3pm) | Afternoon (11-2pm) | Second Afternoon (11am-8pm)

Personal Values

  • Service - Having a heart for taking care of the needs and wants of others.
  • Responsibility - Being personally accountable for actions and behaviors.
  • Excellence - Pursuing nothing short of the best in all areas of life.
  • Respect - Showing honor, dignity, and charity to others in word and deed.
  • Leadership - Equipping and inspiring others to achieve excellence.

Benefits Include

  • Closed on Sundays
  • Flexible Scheduling
  • Scholarship/Tuition Reimbursement Opportunities
  • Competitive Pay
  • 401k + Vacation (Full-time only)*
